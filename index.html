About Me

Hello! I'm Javkhlan Rentsendorj, a professional web designer and developer with 15 years of experience. I've worked on modern web, mobile, and 3D projects, while also lecturing at the National University of Mongolia and serving on the board of the Mongolian Web Developers Association.

As a serial entrepreneur, I've co-founded and led multiple successful startups, including yesh.mn, Octagon, and e-geree.mn. Currently, I'm the Co-Founder and CIO at Powered LLC, where I oversee project planning, IT infrastructure (AWS, Google Cloud), IT policy, strategic partnerships, and pitch our products on major stages.

My startups have earned prestigious recognitions such as ISO27001 certification, Best Startup in Digital Night 2024, Winner Startup Challenge Mongolia 2024, JICA Selected Startup 2024, and EBRD Selected Top 5 Mongolian Startup. These achievements showcase my pitching skill, leadership, and dedication to building world-class solutions.

I'm an open-minded team leader with a passion for building meaningful technology solutions that empower communities and businesses.
